Meet Navigator

Faster

Navigator is much faster than ClickGUI, both FPS-wise and productivity-wise. Some people spend over 45s looking through the ClickGUI searching for a certain mod, but Navigator's integrated search bar can do that for you in a matter of milliseconds.

Easier

Do you want to know what BookHack does or how ServerFinder works? No you can find out! Every feature in Wurst can have a tutorial button on its Navigator page, making it easier than ever to get started with features that you're not familar with.

More Flexible

Mods, Commands and Special Features - Now they're all in one place. And instead of just having a small toggle button, every feature has its own page containing its description, settings, keybinds, and more.

Powerful Search

Navigator's search bar uses ranking by relevance and meta tags to ensure that you get the best possible results. Unlike most in-game search engines, including Minecraft's creative inventory search, Navigator won't simply list the features that have your search term in the title. We even use Google Analytics (if enabled in Wurst Options) to analyze the searches and ensure that everyone gets what they're searching for.

Easy Keybinds

Navigator makes keybinds easy: You just click the "+" button on the keybinds section of a feature, select what the keybind should do and what key should trigger it. Unlike the Keybind Manager, Navigator doesn't require you to remember the command for the thing you want to keybind, making the entire process of keybinding as easy as clicking on a few buttons.

Fully Responsive
